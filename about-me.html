Here you can learn about me as a person

Hello, and welcome to my personal page!

I'm from the city of Warsaw, where I’ve spent most of my life. My journey has been shaped by the people I've chosen to surround myself with, and I believe that my friendships have played a significant role in who I am today.

My love for music began early. For nine years, I dedicated myself to mastering the guitar (here is a link to one of my performances). Starting from primary school, I progressed through music school, reaching the second level before deciding to shift my focus to sports. This transition marked the beginning of a new chapter in my life.

During my high school years and the start of my university studies, I pursued athletics professionally. Specializing in the 200m and 400m sprints, I trained rigorously every day at a professional club in Warsaw. This dedication led to multiple finals at Polish championships and even some medals – achievements that mean a lot to me.

Academically, I chose to study at the Warsaw School of Economics, where I initially majored in business. My involvement in the student government, particularly in organizing the SGH run, was a rewarding experience. I successfully defended my bachelor's thesis in 2023. Currently, I’m still at SGH, delving into Big Data to solidify my technical skills.

Sport remains a significant part of my life. As an ex-athlete, I have an enduring need to train. Currently, I’m preparing for a 1/4 Ironman triathlon, though I often dabble in various other disciplines. My days are filled with more than just work; I immerse myself in personal projects and continually enrich my portfolio.

I have a keen interest in the startup ecosystem, attending events, meetings, and lectures to connect with like-minded individuals. My curiosity often leads me to work on ideas that could potentially evolve into startups.

Outside of my professional and academic pursuits, I cherish spending time with my family and friends, traveling, and diving into good books.

Thanks for taking the time to learn a little bit about me!
